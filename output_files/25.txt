Landed founder is connecting blue-collar workers to better jobs while helping employers decrease turnover
Welcome back to Found where we tell you the stories behind the startups.This week we talk to Landed founder and CEO Vivian Wang who is on a mission to connect blue-collar workers with high-quality job opportunities. Landed handles the hiring process from recruiting to vetting to setting up interviews and facilitating a feedback loop for the general managers to make their workplaces more desirable. They’re also improving employees’ financial well-being by helping them upscale once they’ve landed the job.
